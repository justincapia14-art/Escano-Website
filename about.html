ESCANO

ESCANO: Escape from the Inferno is a survival horror game set in a dark, demon-infested maze where every step could be your last.

Players must navigate narrow corridors, avoid relentless enemies, and search for hidden keys or codes to escape.

With no map to guide you, survival depends on your memory, awareness, and decision-making skills.

WILL YOU FIND YOUR WAY OUT...
OR BE TRAPPED IN THE INFERNO FOREVER?

Survive the depths. Face the Demon Lords. Escape from the Inferno.
