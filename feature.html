SURVIVAL MECHANICS

The Inferno is unforgiving. Master these skills to escape alive.

Walking drains HP

The abyss is hot. Moving drains your HP. Every step brings you closer to death.

Sticky Ceiling

Traps below? Use the sticky feature. Stick and walk seamlessly on the bottom of platforms.

Aim

Aim to the left or right only using left and right click to the mouse to take down enemies from afar. Your accuracy determines your survival.

Unleash the Ultimate

Charge up and unleash Ultimate attack. More Damage to the enemies.

Destroy the Bricks

Dead end? Use your Ultimate skill to smash through breakable bricks.

Soul Collection

Find and collect rare extra lives hidden within the dungeon to extend your run.

The Gatekeeper's Keys

The exit remains sealed until you explore the depths and collect ALL hidden keys.

Survive the depths. Face the Demon Lords. Escape from the Inferno.
