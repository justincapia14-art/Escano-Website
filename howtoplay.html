Master The Mechanics

Learn the basics to survive and escape the Inferno.

Tip 01

Movement & Jumping

Move through a dangerous area. Press A to go left and D to go right. Press W or the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or avoid danger.

Tip 02

Ceiling Stick

Use the surroundings to help you. Jump up to the ceiling to avoid enemies on the ground. Move along the ceiling, then drop down through gaps or holes to get back.

Tip 03

Swap Your Skills

Change skills anytime during battle. Scroll your Mouse Wheel up or down to switch between skills quickly.

Tip 04

Precision Aiming

Aim your attacks with your mouse. Left-Click to attack left, and Right-Click to attack right.

Tip 05

Unleash Your Ultimate

Aim with your mouse. Hold Left-Click or Right-Click for 2 seconds to charge and release a powerful Ultimate attack in that direction.

Tip 06

Break Through Walls

Path blocked? Use your Ultimate on brick walls to break them. Charge it up and clear the way.
